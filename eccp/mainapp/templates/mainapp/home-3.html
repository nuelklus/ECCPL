    Welcome To East Cent Cocoa Processing Plant

    We produce semi-finished cocoa products accustomed and defined cocoa liquor, natural cocoa butter and natural cocoa cake and cocoa powder.By this, we process a range of products from 100% Ghana cocoa beans where total attention is paid to the selection of beans.

    AT The Farm

    We produce one of the best cocoa fruits on our farm due to special practices carried out by our dedicated farmers. We do not compromise on employing best practices on cultivating our cocoa fruits.

    dry Cocoa

    Very cocoa beans are carefully selected and dried to make your semi-finished products. During drying, farmers have to critically observe the process to produce good cocoa beans for further processing. 

    Cocoa Machinery

    We use variety of modern machines to make high quality and tasty cocoa products.

    About Us

    Our Story

    East Cent Cocoa Processing Limited was established in 2020 with the purpose of adding value to our Ghana Cocoa Beans for local and International Community. With the expertise, good management structure and brands and channels, every client or end consumer is assured of good and hygienic products

    Our Vision

    To Produce the Best Cocoa Products from Ghana's Prime Cocoa that Feeds the Local and International Community

    Our Mission

    To Process Ghana's Prime Cocoa for the Local and International Community with Hard Work and Dedication.We Achieve this, by Applying Scientific and Practical Knowledge with State of the Art Technology and Certification that ensure good and Hygienic Products

    Products

    Cocoa Liquor

    Is produce from cocoa beans that are subsequently grinded into paste which can be further pressed into cocoa butter and cocoa cake/powder through pressing mechanism.

    Cocoa Butter

    Is used in chocolate refining as well as creams and ointments and also pharmaceutical industry. Pure cocoa butter pressed from whole roasted cocoa beans- neutral in colour and taste.

    Cocoa Cake

    Is the by ingredients after cocoa butter has been pressed out of Cocoa liquor. The cake can then be pulverized/grinded to make natural or alkalized cocoa powder. We produce both.
